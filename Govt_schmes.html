Government schemes of FPOs

Central Sector Scheme for Formation and Promotion of 10,000 FPOs

Objective: To form and promote 10,000 new FPOs across the country.

Support: Provides financial assistance of up to ₹18 lakh per FPO for a period of 5 years for initial handholding, training, and capacity building.

Implementing Agencies: Small Farmers’ Agribusiness Consortium (SFAC), National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC), National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD), and other entities.

Equity Grant Scheme

Objective: To enhance the equity base of FPOs.

Support: Provides a matching equity grant of up to ₹15 lakh to FPOs to strengthen their financial base.

Credit Guarantee Fund Scheme

Objective: To provide a credit guarantee to lending institutions providing loans to FPOs.

Support: Ensures up to 85% of the loan amount (up to ₹1 crore) taken by FPOs is covered under the guarantee.

Venture Capital Assistance Scheme

Objective: To support FPOs in setting up agribusiness ventures.

Support: Provides financial assistance in the form of venture capital to meet the shortfall in the capital requirement for implementing viable projects.

Formation and Promotion of Farmer Producer Organizations (FPOs) under NABARD

Objective: To promote and nurture FPOs, enabling them to scale up their operations.

Support: Offers capacity building, training, and a range of financial products to support FPOs in their operations.

National Food Security Mission (NFSM) – FPO Promotion

Objective: To support the formation of FPOs in specific crops to increase productivity and ensure better market linkages.

Support:: Provides assistance for the formation of FPOs in select districts and crops.

Mission for Integrated Development of Horticulture (MIDH) – FPO Promotion

Objective:To promote the formation of FPOs in the horticulture sector.

Support:Provides financial assistance for the formation and nurturing of FPOs involved in horticulture activities.

Small Farmers Agribusiness Consortium (SFAC) Support

Objective: To assist FPOs in securing better prices for their products and improving market access.

Support:SFAC acts as a facilitator, helping FPOs with marketing, linkages, and aggregation of produce.

NABARD’s PRODUCE Fund

Objective:To support the promotion and nurturing of FPOs across the country.

Support:Provides funds to Producer Organizations Promoting Institutions (POPIs) to help them create and support FPOs.

National Cooperative Development Corporation (NCDC) Assistance

Objective: To support cooperative societies and FPOs in agribusiness activities.

Support:Provides financial support in the form of loans and grants for the establishment and strengthening of FPOs.

Agri Infrastructure Fund (AIF)

Objective:To facilitate the creation of farm gate infrastructure for FPOs, including warehouses, cold storages, and processing units.

Support:Provides medium to long-term debt financing for investment in viable projects under agriculture infrastructure.

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY) – FPO Support

Objective:To promote the formation of FPOs focused on water use efficiency and irrigation.

Support:Provides financial assistance for the formation of FPOs in water-stressed areas.
